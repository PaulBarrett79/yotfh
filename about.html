Bio

 

Hello! We are a four piece alternative rock / synthpop band from the Liverpool/Manchester area with a sound that has been compared to Phoenix, Metronomy and Pavement.

We are:
Phil Larkin - keyboards and singing
Mike Barrett - singing, bass, guitar
Adam Mayes - on the drums
Bren Collins - singing, guitar, bass

We have been played on the Steve Lamacq "6 Music Recommends" show and have also been declared "Buzz Band Of The Week" by the Liverpool Echo that particular week.
